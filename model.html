Incentives for Submitting Models to GeFi

Developers and data providers submitting models to GeFi receive automated token rewards via smart contracts based on contribution quality and impact, model ownership tokens as proof of participation with governance voting rights, and recurring revenue from licensing or subscriptions in the GeFi AI Model Marketplace, plus ongoing value through usage feedback loops.
