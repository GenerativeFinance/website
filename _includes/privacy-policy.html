1. Definitions

In this Privacy Policy, the following definitions apply:

  • “Personal Data” means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • “Non-Custodial Wallet” means a blockchain wallet under the exclusive control of the User.
  • “Platform” refers to all services provided by GeFi including SaaS applications, Marketplace, Community, and blockchain functionalities.
  • “Services” refers collectively to all features, tools, and applications provided via the Platform.
  • “Processing” means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, storage, analysis, and disclosure.

2. Information We Collect

We collect Personal Data in the following categories:

  • Account Information: name, email address, username, password, and profile details.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, login history, and interaction with Services.
  • Transaction Data: blockchain addresses, transaction records, purchase or sale history, and Marketplace interactions.
  • Communications: emails, support requests, forum postings, and feedback submitted via the Platform.
  • Cookies & Tracking Data: information collected through cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy.

3. Use of Personal Data

We use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain Services, including account management, feature delivery, and technical support.
  • To facilitate Marketplace transactions and Community interactions.
  • To analyze Platform usage for service improvement, analytics, and research.
  • To provide communications, notifications, and service-related updates.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations, fraud prevention, and security measures.
  • To personalize and enhance user experience, including recommendations and feature optimization.

4. Third-Party Services & Blockchain Interactions

GeFi may engage third-party service providers for analytics, cloud hosting, or payment facilitation. Additionally:

  • All blockchain and smart contract interactions are non-custodial; GeFi does not store private keys or control assets.
  • Third-party blockchain services may process transaction data; GeFi is not responsible for their privacy practices.
  • Users remain solely responsible for compliance with local regulations when interacting with blockchain or Marketplace features.

5. User Rights & Choices

Users have the following rights regarding Personal Data:

  • Access and review their Personal Data held by GeFi.
  • Request correction, deletion, or anonymization of Personal Data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.
  • Object to the processing of Personal Data for marketing purposes.
  • Exercise rights under applicable privacy laws, including PDPA or similar regulations.

Requests can be submitted via legal(a)gefi.io. GeFi will respond in accordance with applicable law and within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Data Retention

GeFi retains Personal Data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, to comply with legal obligations, or to enforce agreements. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and regulatory requirements.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Personal Data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. Users acknowledge that use of the Platform is at their own risk.

8. International Data Transfers

Personal Data may be transferred to, stored, or processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including Singapore. By using the Services, you consent to such transfers. GeFi ensures appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age of 18. GeFi does not knowingly collect Personal Data from minors. If we learn that Personal Data of a minor has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

GeFi may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted to the Platform with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
