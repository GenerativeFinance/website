Introduction & Purpose

This Cookie Policy (“Policy”) explains how GeFi Technologies Pte. Ltd. (“GeFi”, “Company”, “we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website, Platform, and related services (“Services”). By accessing or using the Services, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please adjust your cookie preferences or discontinue use of the Services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files or identifiers stored on your device by your browser while accessing a website. Cookies allow the Platform to recognize your device, store preferences, improve performance, and provide personalized experiences. Similar technologies include local storage, web beacons, and pixel tags.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for the operation of the Platform, including authentication, account management, and security. These cookies cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable features such as language preferences, display settings, and interface customizations.
  • Analytics & Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information on Platform usage, traffic sources, and user interactions to improve Services.
  • Marketing & Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements, measure campaign effectiveness, and track engagement. These may include third-party cookies.

3. Cookie Consent & Control

Users have the right to control the use of cookies through their browser settings or platform-provided consent management tools. By continuing to use the Services without changing settings, you consent to the placement and use of cookies as described in this Policy. Users may withdraw consent at any time by modifying browser or device settings.

4. Third-Party Cookies

The Platform may use third-party cookies for analytics, advertising, or performance measurement. GeFi is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party providers. Users are advised to review the third-party privacy and cookie policies for more information.

5. Data Collected via Cookies

Cookies may collect information such as:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type, operating system, and device model
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation behavior
  • Preferences, settings, and feature usage
  • Interactions with marketing or promotional content

All information collected is used in compliance with applicable data protection laws and for the purposes described in this Policy.

6. Retention Periods

Cookies may be session-based (deleted when the browser is closed) or persistent (stored for a specified period). The retention period varies depending on the type of cookie and the purpose for which it is used, generally ranging from a few hours to two years.

7. User Rights & Privacy

Users have the following rights regarding cookie-related data:

GeFi encourages users to regularly review cookie settings and privacy preferences to maintain control over personal information.
