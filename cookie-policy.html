1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone) when you visit a website. They store information about your browsing activity and preferences, enabling features like remembering login details or tracking usage. Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags, local storage, and scripts, which function like cookies.

Cookies can be "session" (temporary, deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" (remain until expired or deleted). They can also be "first-party" (set by us) or "third-party" (set by partners for analytics or advertising).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance functionality, security, and user experience on the Platform. Given our focus on privacy-preserving AI and federated learning, we minimize data collection and ensure compliance with data protection laws. Cookies help us:

  • Provide Essential Services: Enable core features like account authentication, local model agent interactions, and secure aggregation.

We do not use cookies to track you across unrelated sites or sell data. All processing aligns with our zero data retention and end-to-end encryption principles.
